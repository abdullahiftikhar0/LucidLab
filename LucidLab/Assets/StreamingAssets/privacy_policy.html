Privacy Policy

update Last updated: March 1, 2026

LucidLab is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use the LucidLab Student application.

database Information We Collect

  • Account information (name, email, institution) provided during registration
  • Classroom membership and join codes
  • Experiment submissions including interaction data, quiz answers, and completion progress
  • Screen recordings of AR sessions (only when you explicitly start recording)
  • Device and usage analytics for app improvement

visibility How We Use Your Data

  • To provide and maintain the LucidLab learning experience
  • To share your submissions and progress with your instructors
  • To generate performance analytics and scores
  • To improve app functionality and fix bugs

share Data Sharing

Your experiment submissions and grades are visible to the instructor of each classroom you join. We do not sell your personal data to third parties. Data is stored securely using Firebase/Google Cloud infrastructure.

photo_camera Camera & AR Data

LucidLab requires camera access for AR experiences. Camera feed is processed locally on your device for marker detection and is never transmitted to our servers. Screen recordings, when enabled, are stored in your Firebase Storage and accessible only to you and your instructor.

lock Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including encrypted data transmission (TLS), Firebase Authentication, and Firestore security rules to protect your information. Access to your data is restricted to authorized personnel only.

delete Your Rights

  • Request access to your personal data at any time
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Withdraw from classrooms and remove membership data

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at support@lucidlab.app.

child_care Children's Privacy

LucidLab is designed for educational use. If you are under 13, please use the app only under the guidance of a parent, guardian, or instructor. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13 without parental consent.
