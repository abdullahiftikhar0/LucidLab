Frequently Asked Questions

Tap the + button on the Home screen. Enter the 6-character join code your instructor shared, then tap "Join Class". The classroom will appear on your dashboard.
Open a classroom, find the experiment you want, and tap Launch AR. Point your camera at the marker sheet provided by your instructor. The 3D objects will appear once the markers are detected.
If your instructor marks your submission as "Needs Revision", a Retake Experiment button will appear on the experiment card. Tap it to start a new session.
Open the classroom, tap the three-dot menu (⋮) in the top-right corner, and select "Leave Classroom". Your past submissions are preserved even after leaving.
Make sure you're in a well-lit environment. Hold your phone 20–40 cm from the markers. Avoid glare or crumpled sheets. If issues persist, try restarting the experiment from the card.
